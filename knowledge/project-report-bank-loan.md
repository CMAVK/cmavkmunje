# Project Reports, CMA Data and Bank Loans

## What we prepare

- **Project reports / DPR** for term loans and new units
- **CMA data** in the bank's prescribed format for cash credit / OD limits and renewals
- **Loan proposals** with financial projections, DSCR and ratio analysis
- **Sensitivity analysis** showing the bank the proposal survives downside scenarios

As Cost Accountants, bankable financial modelling is a core strength of the firm.

## Documents we need from you

- Last 2–3 years' financial statements and ITRs (for existing businesses)
- Last 6–12 months' bank statements
- GST returns for the recent period
- Quotations for machinery/assets to be purchased (for term loans)
- Existing loan sanction letters and repayment track record
- KYC of promoters and the entity
- Brief note on the business plan — what, where, how much, by when

For brand-new ventures we build projections from your plan and industry benchmarks.

## Government schemes we work with

- **Mudra loans** (up to ₹10 lakh, no collateral)
- **PMEGP** (subsidy-linked for new micro enterprises)
- **CGTMSE** (collateral-free credit guarantee for MSMEs)
- **Stand-Up India** and Maharashtra state schemes (CMEGP)

Eligibility depends on the promoter and project profile — share your plan and
we'll identify the best-fit scheme.

## What bankers scrutinise

Realistic revenue projections, a healthy Debt Service Coverage Ratio,
justified working-capital assessment, promoter contribution (margin), and
consistency between projections, GST data and bank statements. Our reports
are built to answer these questions before the banker asks them.
