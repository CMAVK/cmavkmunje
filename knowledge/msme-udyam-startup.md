# MSME (Udyam), Startup India and Other Registrations

## Udyam (MSME) registration

Free, online and Aadhaar-based. Classification is by investment in plant &
machinery and turnover (micro, small, medium). Benefits include priority
lending, protection against delayed payments (buyers must pay MSMEs within
45 days), interest subvention schemes, and preference in government tenders.

**Documents:** Aadhaar of proprietor/authorised signatory, PAN, GSTIN (where
applicable) and basic business details. Usually completed the same day.

## Startup India (DPIIT) recognition

For eligible private limited companies, LLPs or registered partnerships less
than 10 years old with turnover under ₹100 crore, working on innovation or
improvement of products/services. Benefits include tax exemption under
Section 80-IAC (subject to approval), easier public procurement, self
certification under labour laws and faster IP processing.

**Documents:** Certificate of Incorporation, PAN, brief write-up on the
innovative nature of the business, website/pitch deck if available.
