# About V K Munje & Company

V K Munje & Company is a firm of Cost Accountants based in Pune, established
in 2019 and registered with the Institute of Cost Accountants of India (ICMAI).

## Leadership

The firm is led by Founder & Principal CMA Vicky Munje (ACMA, LLB, M.Com),
supported by CA Deepak Mehta (ACA, Associate) and a professional team.

## Motto
