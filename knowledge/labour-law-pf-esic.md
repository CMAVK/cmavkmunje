# Labour Law Compliance — PF, ESIC and Professional Tax

## When PF registration is mandatory

Establishments with 20 or more employees must register under EPF. Voluntary
registration is possible below that. Contribution applies to employees within
the prescribed wage ceiling (higher-paid employees can be covered by choice).

## When ESIC registration is required

Generally applies to establishments with 10 or more employees (thresholds
vary by state and establishment type) where employees earn up to ₹21,000 per
month (higher for employees with disability). It funds medical care and cash
benefits for employees.

## Professional Tax (Maharashtra)

Employers must register (PTRC/PTEC), deduct professional tax from salaries
as per the slab, and pay the employer's own enrolment tax. Due dates depend
on liability size; annual returns apply.

## Documents needed for PF/ESIC registration

- PAN of the entity and Certificate of Incorporation / registration proof
- Address proof of the establishment
- Details and KYC of employer/directors/partners
- Employee list with date of joining, salary and Aadhaar
- Bank details and digital signature

## Ongoing monthly compliance

- PF: contribution deposit and ECR filing by the 15th of the following month
- ESIC: contribution by the 15th of the following month
- PT: deduction and payment per Maharashtra slab and schedule
