# Company and LLP Registration — Process and Documents

## Choosing the right structure

- **Proprietorship** — simplest, no separate legal identity; suits very small businesses.
- **Partnership firm** — easy to start, partners personally liable.
- **LLP** — limited liability with lighter compliance than a company; suits professionals and small businesses.
- **Private Limited Company** — separate legal entity, preferred for startups seeking funding, ESOPs or scale.

We discuss turnover plans, funding, liability and tax impact before recommending a structure.

## Documents required (directors/partners)

- PAN and Aadhaar of each director/partner
- Photograph, mobile number and email
- Identity proof (passport/voter ID/driving licence)
- Address proof (bank statement/utility bill, recent)
- Registered office proof: utility bill + rent agreement and owner NOC (if rented)

## Process — Private Limited Company (SPICe+)

1. Digital Signatures (DSC) for directors
2. Name reservation (two proposed names)
3. SPICe+ filing — incorporation, PAN, TAN, EPFO/ESIC, professional tax and bank account in one integrated form
4. Certificate of Incorporation with CIN

Typical timeline is about 7–15 working days depending on name approval and
MCA processing.

## Process — LLP (FiLLiP)

Similar flow: DSC → name reservation → FiLLiP incorporation → LLP agreement
filing within 30 days of incorporation.

## After incorporation — don't miss these

- Company: INC-20A (commencement of business) within 180 days, first auditor
  appointment within 30 days, share certificates, statutory registers
- Every year: AOC-4, MGT-7/7A (company) or Form 8 and Form 11 (LLP), DIR-3 KYC for every director
- Missing annual filings attracts per-day additional fees and can lead to
  director disqualification — we maintain a compliance calendar for every
  entity we incorporate.
