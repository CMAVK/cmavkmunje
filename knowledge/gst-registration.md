# GST Registration — Process and Documents

## Who needs GST registration

GST registration is generally required when aggregate turnover crosses the
threshold (commonly ₹40 lakh for goods and ₹20 lakh for services in most
states — lower in special-category states), for anyone making inter-state
taxable supplies, e-commerce sellers, and persons liable under reverse charge.
Voluntary registration is also possible and often useful for claiming Input
Tax Credit and dealing with corporate customers. We confirm applicability for
each client's exact situation before applying.

## Documents required — Proprietorship

- PAN and Aadhaar of the proprietor
- Photograph of the proprietor
- Proof of business address: electricity bill / rent agreement + NOC from owner / ownership document
- Bank details: cancelled cheque or bank statement
- Mobile number and email (OTP verification)

## Documents required — Partnership / LLP

- PAN of the firm/LLP and of all partners
- Partnership deed / LLP agreement and Certificate of Incorporation (LLP)
- Aadhaar and photograph of authorised partner
- Business address proof and bank details as above
- Letter of authorisation for the authorised signatory

## Documents required — Private Limited Company

- PAN of the company, Certificate of Incorporation, MOA/AOA
- PAN, Aadhaar and photographs of directors
- Board resolution / authorisation letter for the authorised signatory
- Registered office address proof and bank details
- Digital Signature (DSC) of the authorised signatory

## Process and timeline

1. We collect and verify your documents.
2. Application is filed on the GST portal with Aadhaar authentication.
3. The department may approve directly or raise a clarification; physical
   verification of premises is possible in some cases.
4. On approval, GSTIN and registration certificate are issued — typically
   within about 7 working days when documents are in order, though department
   timelines can vary.

## After registration

Return filing obligations start immediately (monthly/quarterly), invoice
format must be GST-compliant, and e-invoicing/e-way bill rules may apply
depending on turnover. We set up the complete post-registration compliance
calendar for every client we register.
