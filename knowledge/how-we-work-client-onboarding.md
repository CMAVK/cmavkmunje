# Working With V K Munje & Company — What To Expect

1. **Reach out** — this chat, the Book Consultation page, phone/WhatsApp
   (+91 99220 99970) or an office visit (Mon–Sat, 10 AM – 7 PM).
2. **Free initial discussion** — we understand your requirement and explain
   the scope, documents needed and expected timeline.
3. **Quotation** — professional fees depend on scope and complexity; you get
   a clear quotation before any work starts, so there are never surprises.
4. **Document collection** — send documents through this chat's 📎 button,
   the website's Upload Documents page, WhatsApp or email.
5. **Execution and updates** — we keep you informed at each milestone and
   share every acknowledgment/filing proof for your records.
